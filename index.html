How to play?(instructions):

For many years, players of all ages have delighted in the timeless casual game Whack-a-Gopher. The goal of the game is to hammer as many moles as you can; but, if you happen to hit the bomb, the game is over. Moles and bombs randomly appear out of holes in a board that is used to play the game. To hit the moles as they appear, players need quick reflexes and good hand-eye coordination.

The player must wait for the moles to emerge from their holes as the game begins with them hidden within. Once a mole appears, the player must use the mallet to strike it fast before it retreats back into its hole.The player receives 10+ points for each mole struck, and the game is over when you unintentionally hit a bomb. The player with the most points at the conclusion of the game week is declared the overall champion, and the score should be posted on Twitter.

The simplicity of Whack-a-Gopher is one of its best features. Everyone of any age or ability level can enjoy the game because it is simple to learn and play. It is also very replayable because it is difficult to predict when and where the moles and explosives will come because they appear in different holes and at varied intervals.

In general, Whack-a-Gopher is a time-tested classic that has endured. Its straightforward yet difficult gameplay makes it a game that players of all ages and backgrounds may enjoy. It's a game that never gets old and gives gamers all across the world hours of entertainment.
